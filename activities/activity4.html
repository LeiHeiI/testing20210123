Intel ISEF

Grade 10 | Phoenix, Arizona

May 2019

Here I am at Phoenix! The place that I have been dreaming about since secondary school. Here I am at Intel ISEF! Finally I reach here after getting through so many troubles and failures. Here I am!!!

Together with the officers from Macao Education Bureau, we set off for Phoenix! I am one of the six Macao finalists and we have three projects in total.

Arrival

After having an extremely long flight, we arrived at Phoenix at night. We are starving, so our teacher brings us to a bar(it is the only restaurant opened near our hotel).

We enjoyed cheese burger and of course ICED LEMON TEA, aka the “most popular drink” in the bar lollll (just ignore the BEER sign on our photo, we had lemon tea instead lol )

Opening Ceremony

The weather is so nice today! We have our opening ceremony in the morning and pin-exchange event at night (btw I met Daniel Kang again, he is also the finalist this year!!)

Getting ready

As usual, we set up our booth and poster. And we start our numerous rehearsals and practices. We have got 2 days for practicing and we made use of them effectively. (The YouTube video is a recording of our rehearsal, sorry we often burst into laughter loll)

Judging Day

We are so nervous before the judges come, but surprisingly we quickly calm down as soon as we start our presentation. Today we chat with 14 judges in total and we are really exhausted, but we try our best to stay enthusiastic and excited during the whole process.

Overall, we think that we have done a pretty good job! Hope that the result is satisfactory….I am so nervous….

Public Day

I met my old friend in the Public Day!! He is Chen Bo Rong from Taiwan and we two met in 2019 TISF!! It is so nice to see him again. Wish him all the best!!!

Grand Awards Ceremony

We didn’t get any Special Award yesterday and I was kind of disappointed. My heart is beating so fast when the host is announcing the winners, but I feel that all my efforts and tears have paid off when my name is announced through the speaker!!!

My dream comes true!!! I got a Grand Third Prize in Engineering Mechanics!!! I feel so thankful for all the support and love I received from my team, my school and my family.

Return

Right after we return to Macao, the Chief Executive of the Macao Education Bureau and our school principals are waiting for us at the wharf. We cheer, we cry, I feel that this is the best day in my life so far.

(In Photo 3, the reason why the boy always opens his mouth while taking photos is that he wants his chin to look slimmer lol)

Highest gratitude to my instructor Mr. Min Hao Nian and my teammate Oliver Fong. I can achieve nothing without your help and support.
