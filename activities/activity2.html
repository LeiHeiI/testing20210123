FIRST Robotics Competition

Grade 8 | Honolulu, Hawaii

April 2017

This was the first time for me to visit the US and I was super excited!!!

My role in this competition was to do all promotional work and make a trip itinerary. During preparation, I made many team badges and leaflets. I planned to distribute them for team promotion.

I contacted four local teams in Hawaii on my own, and I felt so grateful that we could have a chance to visit their campus and communicate with their teammates.

Until now, we still keep in touch with each other through Instagram and share our experience in robotics!
