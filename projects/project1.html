Chinese Dougong brackets

Grade 10 | Computer Club

Winter 2019

Dougong bracket is an ancient Chinese building structure that really blows my mind!

It acts to transfer the stress from rooftop to the main pillar and to ensure the overall stability of the rooftop. It was invented in the Warring States period and has served for more than 2500 years!

This structure is a really complicated one, so I first drew a 2D draft before building it.
