FTC robot

Grade 8 | Inner-school FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC)

Winter 2017

From 3D design to programming, this is the first robot which I played a vital role in every step of its construction process. This is a robot for 2016-2017 Velocity Vortex Game. It shoots balls and presses beacons to score in the game.

My interest in the amazing power of engineering first formed when I read a comic book about steam engines during my childhood. The way in which engineers make use of their creativity to improve people’s lives is fascinating. In particular, my aim is to study Mechanical Engineering because it is essential knowledge included in almost every engineering project, which is the sole driving force to make the world a better place.
